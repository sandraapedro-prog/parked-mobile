What is PARKED.?

PARKED. helps you store and revisit unfinished or future intentions, like creative ideas or hobbies. It is not a productivity tool (no streaks or pressure), not a social platform (everything stays on your device), and not a to-do list (items never expire).

Core concepts:

- Park = save something for later
- Unpark = return to it when ready

Target users: Anyone who collects hobbies and ideas faster than they finish them.

Park an item — give it a title, description, category, energy level (low/medium/high), and an optional check-in date.

Dashboard — see all your parked ideas at a glance, with a “Due for Check-in” section highlighting what’s gently asking for your attention soon.

Stats — total count, how many are parked vs. active vs. released.

Item detail — view, edit, revive (un-pause) an item, snooze its check-in date, or let it go (delete).
